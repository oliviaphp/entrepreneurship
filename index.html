OUR APP FEATURES

With our wide range of features, we offer all the tools you need to easily post and sign up for tasks.

Post Tasks

Anyone can post a task. Posters will be required to input information, such as the amount of money offered, the task description and title, the task location, and the task time, date and duration. A poster can add a task as early as one week in advance of the task date.

Sign-Up For Tasks

Students can sign up for as many tasks as they like. They can easily keep track of their past and future tasks through the “My Tasks” section and even leave a review for the poster.

Tasks Map

The Tasks Map shows a student where there are tasks in a given area. The student filter the results, such as the time and length of the task, the amount being offered for a task, the task date and the task type. They can also enter a keyword that can help to filter tasks. Red tasks are tasks available within 24 hours, while green tasks are posted up to a week in advance.

Messaging

UniTasker provides a convenient messaging system where job posters can get in touch with reliable students. Taskers and posters can only be in contact if the task has been accepted and confirmed.

FAQ

What is UniTasker?

UniTasker offers a way for students who don’t have time for part-time employment to make some pocket money by doing tasks between lectures or on weekends. The app allows people to post tasks they need done and then verified students can click on the task and request to complete the task. Once the poster confirms the tasker based on their profile, they can contact the student via a messenger system and the task can be arranged. Payment is release to the student via PayPal after the task has been marked as complete, from which a small percentage is taken as a UniTasker fee.

How do I setup this App?

All you have to do is download the app on either the Apple Store, Google Play, or Windows Store. Once you have downloaded the app, all you need to is register your details. Students will be asked to provide a university email so that their student status can be verified.

Does it cost anything to become a member?

No - UniTasker is completely free to use.

Who can post tasks?

Anyone can post a task. All they need to do is sign up to UniTasker and then they can post as many tasks as they like!

Who can complete tasks?

Only university students can complete tasks. When a student signs up for UniTasker, there will be an email verification process. Therefore, if you wish to sign up as a tasker, please make sure that you register with your university email. By completing tasks, UniTasker allows students to not only get to know other people, but to earn money while doing it. Through UniTasker, students can work as much as they want, when they want.
